1. Button Logic & Press Mapping

Button 1 GPIO 22
In Menu (Quick Tap):Value + (Mode, Time, Sets)
In Menu (Hold 2s):(No Action)
During Session (Tap):(No Action)
Button 2 GPIO 23
In Menu (Quick Tap):Next Screen > (Press on last screen to Start)
In Menu (Hold 2s):(No Action)
During Session (Tap):(No Action)
Button 3 GPIO 24
In Menu (Quick Tap):Value - (Mode, Time, Sets)
In Menu (Hold 2s):(No Action)
During Session (Tap):(No Action)
Button 4 GPIO 25
In Menu (Quick Tap):< Previous Screen
In Menu (Hold 2s):QUIT PROGRAM (Acts like Ctrl+C)
During Session (Tap):STOP SESSION (Returns to menu)

2. LCD Display & Color-Chip Mapping

Green - Move/Exercise
Blue - Stay/Rest
White - Settings
Cyan - Ready
Orange - Paused
Red - Stopped
Magenta - Complete
Gray - Quit
